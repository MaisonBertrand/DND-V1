House Rules & Lore

Custom House Rules

  • Luck Points: Each player receives 1 Luck Point per session, usable for rerolls or to avoid disaster.
  • Short Rest Healing: You regain half your level (rounded up) in hit points after a short rest, in addition to normal healing.
  • Critical Hits: On a natural 20, double all damage dice and add a flavorful effect.

World Lore

Eldoria is a world shaped by the ancient conflict between the Sunlords and the Moonshadow cult. Magic pulses through the land, and forgotten ruins await discovery by brave adventurers.

Factions

  • The Sunspire Monastery: Guardians of light and knowledge.
  • Moonshadow Cult: Secretive wielders of forbidden magic.
  • The Silver Company: Mercenaries and monster hunters for hire.

Notable Locations

  • Bramblewood: A peaceful village now on edge.
  • Whispering Forest: Enchanted woods filled with peril.
  • Stormkeep: Crumbling fortress haunted by the past.
